    • Apa itu GOOD FOOD ??

    Since of 2022, Good Food being a website that dedicated to helping restaurants, who want to donate their quality food that has not yet been sold, Good Food works to serve the community, providing humanitarian aid in the form of food to meet needs. Apart from that, Good Food is dedicated to making quality food available from restaurants that provide it. In this way, the food provided will be more useful because it is not wasted.





    Tujuan Kami

    "Menjadi agen perubahan utama dalam meminimalkan pemborosan makanan, memberdayakan komunitas, dan mendukung kesejahteraan sosial serta lingkungan melalui penyaluran makanan sisa dari restoran. Kami berkomitmen untuk menciptakan dunia di mana setiap orang memiliki akses terhadap makanan yang bergizi, sambil menjaga keberlanjutan lingkungan."
